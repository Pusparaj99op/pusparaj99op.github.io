I'm a passionate Computer Science Engineering student specializing in game development and cybersecurity.

I'm Kalvin Shah, a Computer Science Engineering student at KDK College of Engineering, Nagpur. Born on September 6, 2005, I'm passionate about technology, cybersecurity, and game development.
